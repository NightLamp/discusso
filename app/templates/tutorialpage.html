What is discusso?

Our website provides a platform for users to express their opinions on arguments and receive responses. Arguments implement an algorithm that calculates public opinion on the argument, based on factors such as up/down votes, user responses and up/down votes on those.

To understand how to use and operate our website, please follow the guide below to show your how to get started.
